Wellness

Spa & Wellness

Salt-bromine-iodine hyperthermal waters (wellhead ~75°C) for medical treatments, relaxation and regeneration.

🌊

Thermal Pools

Indoor pool connected to two outdoor pools in the park. Temperatures ~34–35°C and one pool at ~30°C. Hydromassage, water games and Kneipp path. Swimming cap mandatory. Hours: 08:00–19:30, Friday and Saturday until 22:30.

🪨

Thermal Caves

Biocave, steam cave, thermal waterfalls, emotional showers with aroma and chromotherapy, Turkish bath and biosauna.

💆

Covered Thermal Treatments

Mud therapy, inhalations, aerosol, balneotherapy, ozonized bath and physiotherapy performed by expert staff. SSN agreement.

✨

Day Spa

Access pools and thermal caves even just for the day, without overnight stay. Changing rooms with showers, hairdryer and locker. Evening entry Friday and Saturday 18:00–22:30. Online booking mandatory.
